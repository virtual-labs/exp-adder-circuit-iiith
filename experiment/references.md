### Textbooks

**1. Digital Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Chapter 4 (Combinational Logic)<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of adder circuits, half adders, full adders, and carry propagation._

**2. Digital Logic and Computer Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano - Chapter 4<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of digital logic design including binary arithmetic and adder implementations._

**3. Digital Electronics: Principles, Devices and Applications**<br/>
BY: Anil K. Maini - Chapter 5<br/>
_Practical approach to digital circuit design with detailed analysis of arithmetic circuits._

**4. Digital Systems: Principles and Applications**<br/>
BY: Ronald J. Tocci, Neal S. Widmer, and Gregory L. Moss - Chapter 6<br/>
_Excellent coverage of digital arithmetic operations and circuit implementation._

**5. Computer Organization and Design**<br/>
BY: David A. Patterson and John L. Hennessy - Chapter 3<br/>
_Hardware perspective on arithmetic operations and their implementation in processors._
